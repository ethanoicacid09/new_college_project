Our Story

Founded in 2018 by wellness enthusiasts Sarah Chen and Marcus Rodriguez, ZenHerb was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that nature holds the keys to our wellbeing.

After years of searching for authentic, high-quality herbs and struggling to find comprehensive yoga guidance, they decided to create the solution they wished existed.

Today, ZenHerb serves over 10,000 customers worldwide, offering premium organic herbs and transformative yoga courses that nurture body, mind, and spirit.
